Parametric Engine

HOW GIGSHIELD
WORKS

No claim filing. No proof of loss. No waiting. The moment a verified disruption event is detected — money moves automatically.

01

Real-Time Trigger Detection

Our trigger engine polls 6+ external APIs every 15 minutes — weather, AQI, traffic, curfew alerts, platform status. When a parametric condition is met (e.g. rainfall > 50mm/6hr), the event is flagged and dual-source verified within 5 minutes.

02

AI Fraud Check + Policy Match

All active policies in the affected zone are matched instantly. Our 17-layer fraud engine runs sensor fusion, cell tower geography, device fingerprinting, and behavioral analysis in parallel. Legitimate claims are cleared in under 3 minutes.

03

Instant Payout + Blockchain Log

Razorpay UPI transfer initiated. Money hits the rider's wallet in under 15 minutes from event detection. Every payout is simultaneously logged on Ethereum testnet — immutable, verifiable, zero disputes. WhatsApp notification sent automatically.

Intelligence Layer

AI/ML THAT
POWERS IT ALL

Every premium, every trigger, every fraud score is computed by machine learning models trained on real Indian weather and income data.

🧠

XGBoost Risk Scorer

Hyper-personalized weekly premium per rider. 12+ features: zone flood-risk, monsoon seasonality, shift hours, delivery platform, historical disruption frequency. Retrains on live claim data.

XGBoost · scikit-learn
🔍

Isolation Forest Anomaly Detector

Unsupervised ML identifies unusual claim patterns — temporal bursts, GPS clusters, multi-account rings — without human labeling. Catches fraud patterns that rule-based systems miss entirely.

Isolation Forest · DBSCAN

Predictive Claims Forecaster

TensorFlow model predicts next week's claim probability per zone using 7-day weather forecasts. Feeds the insurer dashboard with advance warning — shifting from reactive claims to proactive risk management.

TensorFlow · Keras
📰

Social Signal NLP
